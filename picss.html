It's you, my Aphrodite


In a garden where the wild blooms sway,

She walks like sunlight, turning night to day.

With every step, the flowers blush and sigh,

Her laughter dances on the breeze, a sigh.


Glimmers of gold weave through her flowing hair,

Each strand a thread of love beyond compare.

Her gaze, a gentle tide, pulls hearts to shore,

Like waves that kiss the sand forevermore.


She holds the essence of a thousand dreams,

A vision wrapped in soft and silver beams.

With every smile, the stars begin to glow,

A symphony of light in twilight's flow.


Oh, Aphrodite, in this world divine,

In your embrace, the cosmos intertwine.

To adore you is to touch the sky so wide,

My heart, forever anchored by your side.
